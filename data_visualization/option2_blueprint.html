About

What I do

I'm a Master's candidate in Computational Analysis and Public Policy at the University of Chicago, where I work at the intersection of data science, economics, and public policy. I build models and pipelines that turn raw, messy data — satellite imagery, century-old archives, property tax records — into evidence people can actually use to make decisions.
